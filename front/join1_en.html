Registrations will open on the 14th of March

Because the Italian Burning Boots is being fully funded by the participants we ask everyone to make a donation during the registration process, the money are used for mountain hut, the food and all the material for the event. For this reason the registration price for the whole event (whether you participate for 1 or 4 days, of course we hope 4) is:

Price for the mountain hut € 80.00

We have a limited space for those who prefer to sleep in a tent, in which case you'll need to bring your tent but you can make use of the bathrooms of the mountain hut and share with everyone all event meals (even sharing the preparation of course). In this case the entry is reduced to:

Price for camping tent € 65.00

 
IMPORTANT: The begin of the registration process is Monday 14th of March 2016 at 6:30 AM CET (Italy)

Low income registrations

We have also decided to offer a very limited number of discounted registrations for those with severe financial difficulties. Such registrations will cost:

Price for the mountain hut - low income € 50.00
Price for camping tent - low income € 35.00

In case you are in severe financial difficulties please write to info@burningboots.it explaining your situation in the best possible way and we will try to find a way to allow everyone to participate. The deadline to apply for low income registration is Thursday 10th of March 2016 at 1:00PM CET

 
IMPORTANT: We can't offer refunds. The low income registration CAN'T be resold to someone else through the forum.
