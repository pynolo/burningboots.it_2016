The principles

Participation

It's not an event for spectators. Get involved, be a volunteer, create and organize. IBB will be different if you are part of it.

Self-reliance

Pack everything you need in your backpack. You are responsible for yourself, mentally and physically. Give yourself what you need - and ask others for help when you need it.

Leave no trace

We don't leave traces of our presence. The area will be left like before the event, or possibly even cleaner. Take all the bags you might need to bring everything back to the car, especially your garbage.

Gifting

We give our time, effort and gifts freely. We give to help others and because it makes us feel good. A workshop, a small badge, a performance... our gifts are from our heart.

No commerce

Forget about money - there's nothing to buy. By removing commerce from our community, we create co-operation and participation.

Self-expression

Be the person you are. Be the person you want to be. We respect and value you, and expect the same from you. You have the freedom to be yourself. Become who you are.

Inclusion

We welcome everyone for their unique contribution to our community. Include others as you want to be included, with respect, consideration and tolerance.

Community & co-operation

A family of individuals, we look after each other. We are united in our need to be part of something larger than ourselves.

Immediacy

Make "now"count. All this will soon be gone, so enjoy now. Experience, participate, be. This is all there is, so seize the moment!
