Regulation - FAQ

How meals will be served?

All meals are included and will be prepared and served by ourself, this means you too

I Can’t come anymore, what should I do?

You can look for someone to replace you! Go in to the Forum in the appropriate section.

Give him/her your replacement code so he/she can change the details on the Registration page.
Did you forget your replacement code? Write at info@burningboots.it

What is "Leave No Trace"??

LNT means we are going to leave the place as we found it when we arrived, without leaving evidence of our stay. Taking care of the environment, being careful about our trash.

Could I reach the retreat by car or motorbike?

Park regulation stipulates that all vehicles must be left at the visitors parking lot. Write at greeters@burningboots.it for transportation of people with any kind of disability.

What about music?

Please bring your own music instruments! You are more than welcome to play them during the event! Just remind to respect other people will by adjusting the volume. Sound systems are not allowed, except special request authorized before the event. For further information and to bring your music performance write at musicart@burningboots.it

Is alcohol allowed?

Sure, but probably won’t included. Bring whatever you would like to find at IBB, with some more for the others. Always remember that you will responsible for yourself, don’t ruin the party.

Will children participate?

Of course, children will participate! Be aware that no specific activities will take place and nobody will be in charge of looking after them.
If you are looking to bring minors please contact info@burningboots.it before subscription.

Can I bring my dog?

We evaluated for a long time if we could also bring our dogs, we discussed with the owners of the retreat and we considered the park's regulations carefully. Unfortunately, at the moment, is not allowed to dogs to stay inside or outside the retreat. We are aware that for some people this will be a limit, so we are considering to perform the future events in a different location.

Can I suggest a different place for the event?

Of course you can! All suggestions are very welcome! Write at info@burningboots.it
Remember anyway that we could move upcoming events only if some volunteers will be able to make all necessary inspections.

Can I take pictures?

You can take pictures just with the explicit consent of the "subject". We will have a specific area in the Forum to exchange pictures between us privately.

Will excursions be difficult?

We are going to choose different types of walks, avoiding the most dangerous. Said that, be ready to walk a lot!

What’s happening if it rains?

The retreat is safety accessible also with rain. Inside we will have enough space to make things happens anyway.

Who is making money out of this?

Absolutely nobody. The only purpose of the minimum donation for subscription is to cover expenses. Money in advance is generally used for the upcoming edition or for similar not commercial events organized by the Italian Burners. Balance will be published on this website after the event.
