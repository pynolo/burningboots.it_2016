What is IBB?

Burn Baby Burn

IBB is an unique event created by the community of Italian Burners to relive some of the aspects of Nowhere and Burning Man.

Excursions and activities will take place to have fun and meet new people. Our temporary home will be a wonderful mountain retreat.

Everyone Involved

No one is audience! Everyone contribute to make IBB possible! This kind of event works only if everyone is willing to do its best. That doesn’t mean working while others have fun, but helping out while everyone else do the same!

Activities

During the event is possible to get involved in different types of activities like excursions, performances and workshops. Bring your most extravagant clothes or express yourself in the best possible way! All activities are not compulsory, no pressure, but if you want live IBB at its best is better to partecipate and get involved!

Inclusion

Inclusion is a "must" and we are working hard to make IBB as inclusive as possible. Children are welcome and this year we are offering different conditions for subscriptions to offer everyone a chance to come and enjoy IBB with us.
The location is near 4 airports, not far away from A1 and A14 and less than 20’ minutes driving from the train station.
