Cosa portare... e cosa no

An essential checklist

We'll be on the top of the mountains, so don't forget WARM clothes.
Some things shouldn't be missing in your backpack! Be careful, items marked with are literally vital!

  • Sleeping bag
  • Good hiking shoes and thick socks*
  • Flashlight
  • Camping mug**
  • Camping dish
  • Camping fork, knife & spoon
  • Water bottle
  • Wet weather gear
  • Sun screen protection & hat
  • Soap & towel
  • Toothbrush & toothpaste
  • Earplugs
  • Sleeping mask
* thin socks usually cause blisters
** use a snap-hook to easilly bring it with you

Cosa NON si può portare

  • No pets (forbidden by the Park rules)
  • No wrong attitude :)

No to everything that's difficult to clean:

  • No glitter
  • No feathers
  • No confetti
  • No explosives or aerial flares

Divieti

  • Unauthorized cars and motorbikes are not allowed in the Park
  • Pets are not allowed in the Park
  • It's forbidden to damage and litter the place
