What to bring

An essential checklist

Weather on the top of the Appennine could be extreme even in June! So be prepared for warm days and cold nights.
This isn’t an exhaustive list, but should give you a good idea.
Be aware, items marked with are literally vital!

  • Sleeping bag
  • Good hiking shoes and thick socks*
  • Flashlight
  • Camping mug**
  • Camping dish
  • Camping fork, knife & spoon
  • Water bottle
  • Wet weather gear
  • Sun screen protection & hat
  • Soap & towel
  • Toothbrush & toothpaste
  • Earplugs
  • Sleeping mask
* thin socks usually cause blisters
** use a snap-hook to easilly bring it with you

What not to bring

  • Glitter
  • Feathers
  • Confetti
  • Explosives
  • Aerial flares
  • Pets (see the FAQ)
  • A bad attitude!
  • It’s also forbidden to drive any kind of unauthorized vehicle through the park
